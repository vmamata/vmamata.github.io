Dr. Mamata Vegunta Singh

About

An artist and a writer, Dr. Mamata Vegunta Singh explores the intersections of psychology and philosophy. She is a senior management professional in the IT industry and partners with the industry and the government on problems pertaining to sustainability and contemporary issues faced by women. As a coach and a mentor, she also teaches at industry forums and management colleges.
